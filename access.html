Making smart contracts accessible.

Easy Access

The first goal for access is to make access as easy as possible. Technologies are already pretty accessible as most smart contract systems are free and open source, but stil barriers to entry and adoption exist. Thus easy access means that access to smart contract technologies in general and specific implementations and DApps is kept open and is facilitated for example through the provision of a 'playground' and testnets.

Accessibility

Bitrights would like to facilitate accessibility of the contracts and DAOs through establishing a bridge between digital/smart structures and legal contracts. This can be provided for by different technical and non-technical means, such as a bridge between code with its documentation and legal texts, visual representation of smart contracts or a new, unified markup language integrating digital, legal and social contracts.
