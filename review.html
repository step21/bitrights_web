Best-Practices for smart contracts.

Fresh Commentary

Through our professional involvement and general interests, we are always keeping up to date with developments in the area of smart contracts and the tech world. If a development seems relevant to smart contracts, we will evaluate it and provide appropriate commentary if we feel it is relevant and has merit..

In-depth review

Apart from fresh commentary, we also like to do in-depth review of new, important and exciting developments. This can be anything from a new whitepaper to a review of a paper, lecture or blogpost. In reviews or papers, we focus on the legal and technical sides and aim to provide comprehensive legal and technical review on an academic, but generally relevant level.

Multi-disciplinary consultation

Though out primary disciplines so far are the legal sciences and technology, we are open to include findings and methodologies from other disciplines such as economics, social and political sciences and the natural sciences into out reviews and consultations.
